# Course 01 Civic Awakening Course Plan

## Course Purpose

Introduce the problem of civic disengagement and show how local participation shapes democracy.

## Chapter Overview

### Chapter 01

Focus: Foundational concept

**Book Narrative Idea**

- Describe the civic concept and why it matters in Arkansas.

**Arkansas Story Example**

- Insert historical case study (e.g., Elaine Massacre, labor movement, ballot initiative fights).

**Course Simulation**

- Create a decision scenario related to the topic.

**Workbook Exercise**

- Ask learners to apply the concept to their own community.

**Workshop Activity**

- Facilitate group discussion or movement planning exercise.
