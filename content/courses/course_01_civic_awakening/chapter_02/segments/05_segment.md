# County Government and Quorum Courts

## Book Narrative

County governments play an important role in Arkansas civic life.

Each county operates through a legislative body known as the quorum court.

Justices of the peace represent districts across the county.

Together they manage county budgets, roads, and public services.

Despite this influence, quorum courts remain one of the least understood institutions in Arkansas government.
