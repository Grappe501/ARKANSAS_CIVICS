# The Arkansas Civic Power Map

## Book Narrative

The Arkansas civic system can be visualized as a layered structure.

Community networks at the base.

Local government above them.

State government beyond that.

Direct democracy tools interacting with the entire system.

Understanding this map helps citizens identify where influence is possible.
