# Direct Democracy

## Book Narrative

Arkansas citizens possess a powerful tool that many Americans do not realize exists.

Through ballot initiatives and referendums, citizens can propose laws or repeal legislation directly at the ballot box.

This system allows voters to bypass the legislature and place issues directly before the public.

## Course Activity

Learners examine a past Arkansas ballot initiative.
