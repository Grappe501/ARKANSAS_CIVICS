# Statewide Executive Power

## Book Narrative

The governor and statewide constitutional officers play a major role in shaping policy.

They implement laws, manage state agencies, and influence legislative priorities.

Executive leadership often determines how laws are applied across the state.

## Course Activity

Learners identify Arkansas executive offices.

## Workbook Exercise

List three statewide officials and their roles.

## Workshop Activity

Discuss how executive power shapes policy outcomes.

## Facilitator Notes

Explain the balance between legislative and executive power.
