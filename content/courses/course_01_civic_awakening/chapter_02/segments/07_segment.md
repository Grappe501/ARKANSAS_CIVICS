# The Arkansas Legislature

## Book Narrative

At the state level, the Arkansas General Assembly writes the laws that govern the state.

The legislature consists of the House of Representatives and the Senate.

These lawmakers debate and pass legislation affecting taxes, education, health policy, and countless other areas.

Citizens influence these decisions through elections, testimony, and advocacy.
