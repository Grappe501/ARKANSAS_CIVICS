# School Boards

## Book Narrative

School boards control decisions that shape the education of thousands of Arkansas children.

They oversee budgets, hire superintendents, and set district policies.

Because schools affect families directly, school board meetings often become key spaces for civic participation.

## Course Activity

Learners examine a school board agenda.

## Workbook Exercise

Identify the members of your school board.

## Workshop Activity

Discussion about education decisions in local communities.

## Facilitator Notes
