# Courts and Legal Power

## Book Narrative

Courts interpret laws and resolve disputes about their meaning.

In Arkansas, legal challenges often determine whether policies remain in place or are overturned.

Court decisions have shaped civil rights, voting rules, and ballot initiative processes.

## Course Activity

Learners examine a case where courts affected public policy.

## Workbook Exercise

Research one Arkansas Supreme Court decision affecting civic life.

## Workshop Activity

Discuss the role of courts in democratic systems.

## Facilitator Notes

Highlight the judiciary as a check on power.
