# Modern Ballot Campaigns

## Book Narrative

In recent decades, Arkansas voters have used the ballot initiative process to address issues ranging from minimum wage increases to medical marijuana legalization.

These campaigns required extensive grassroots organizing and statewide coalition building.

## Course Activity

Analyze how ballot campaigns gather signatures.
