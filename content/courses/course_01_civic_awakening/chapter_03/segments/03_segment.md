# The Promise and Limits of Reform

## Book Narrative

The populist movement promised to expand democratic participation.

But the South, including Arkansas, remained deeply shaped by racial divisions.

Many reform movements struggled with the tension between expanding economic democracy while confronting systems of racial exclusion.

These tensions would define much of Arkansas civic history in the decades that followed.

## Course Activity

Analyze tensions between reform and exclusion.

## Workbook Exercise

Write about how reform movements sometimes contain contradictions.

## Workshop Activity

Discuss why some movements expand democracy while others restrict it.
