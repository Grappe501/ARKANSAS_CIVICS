# Messaging and Public Narrative

## Book Narrative

Movements succeed not only because of policy ideas but because of the stories they tell.

Public narratives shape how people understand problems and solutions.

Effective civic campaigns communicate clearly, emotionally, and consistently.

## Course Activity
