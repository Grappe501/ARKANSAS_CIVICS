# Coalition Building

## Book Narrative

Successful civic campaigns rarely operate alone.

Coalitions bring together organizations, community leaders, and volunteers with shared goals.

These partnerships allow movements to expand their reach and legitimacy.

## Course Activity

Learners identify groups that might form a coalition.

## Workbook Exercise

List organizations in your community that share similar concerns.

## Workshop Activity

Build a hypothetical coalition map.

## Facilitator Notes

Emphasize relationships over ideology.
