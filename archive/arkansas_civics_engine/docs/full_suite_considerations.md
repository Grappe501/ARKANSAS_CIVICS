# Full Suite Considerations

In addition to the book, course, workbook, workshop, and facilitator guide, consider:

1. **Assessment bank**
   Shared quiz items, reflection prompts, scenario questions, and evaluation rubrics.

2. **Source library**
   A structured archive for primary documents, research sources, press clips, and citations.

3. **Asset registry**
   Maps, charts, photos, icons, diagrams, downloadable handouts, and slide graphics.

4. **Story archive**
   County-level stories, organizing stories, interviews, and campaign case studies tied to lessons.

5. **Simulation engine**
   Structured decisions, branching scenarios, and scoring states for movement-building exercises.

6. **Data warehouse layer**
   County demographics, labor stats, ballot initiative history, elected officials, and district maps.

7. **Evaluation layer**
   Workshop feedback, learner progress, organizer follow-up, and conversion to real civic action.

8. **Appendix/field manual layer**
   Downloadable organizer packets, host kits, and tactical reference documents.
