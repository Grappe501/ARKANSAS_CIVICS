## Canonical Body

Arkansas citizens have unusual power through initiatives and referendums. These tools matter because they allow people to act when the legislature will not, or to challenge major laws after passage. They are not easy tools. They require signatures, timing, legal precision, and public trust. But they are real tools.

## Workbook Prompt

Write one example of an issue better suited to an initiative and one better suited to a referendum.

## Workshop Activity

Facilitator presents three hypothetical issues. Participants decide which route—legislative lobbying, initiative, or referendum—fits each one best.

## Facilitator Notes

Stress that direct democracy is both empowering and contested.

## Course Interaction

Knowledge check: Which tool is used to challenge a law the legislature already passed?
