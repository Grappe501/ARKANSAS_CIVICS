## Canonical Body

In Arkansas, local government decisions shape daily life more directly than many statewide debates. City councils affect zoning, development, policing, and budgets. County quorum courts oversee county budgets, roads, and services. School boards shape education policy, district leadership, and student priorities.

These spaces are often the first rooms where citizens can move from frustration to influence.

## Workbook Prompt

List the city, county, and school entities that affect your household most directly.

## Workshop Activity

Participants build a three-column chart: city, county, school. Under each, list what decisions are made there.

## Facilitator Notes

If participants do not know their institutions, that ignorance is not failure. It is a sign of how inaccessible civic systems have become.

## Course Interaction

Scenario: A development project near your neighborhood is approved with little notice. Which local body likely handled the earliest discussion?
