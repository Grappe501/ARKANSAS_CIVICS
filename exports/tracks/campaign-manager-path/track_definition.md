# Campaign Manager Path

A systems-level leadership pathway for designing, staffing, and executing campaigns.

- Slug: `campaign-manager-path`
- Audience: Campaign managers, field directors, chiefs of staff, organizers moving into leadership.
- Courses: 8
- Estimated hours: 20.8
- Tags: campaigns, strategy, leadership, management

## Description

Build the full civic and operational stack needed to lead local or statewide campaigns in Arkansas, including strategy, narrative, field systems, rule changes, and long-term sustainability.
